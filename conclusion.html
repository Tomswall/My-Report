The following report will document the process of learning two new coding languages and then, using this new knowledge, build and publish my own public, personal website.

In the current age having a personal website is a valuable asset to every company, business and individual looking to promote themselves and their work. Having the skills to build a website yourself would be very useful, allowing you to save money and provide something to future employers that not many can. It is hence why I have chosen to do this as my personal project; not only does it fulfill the criteria needed for this assignment but is also an opportunity to learn a useful, real-life skill.
